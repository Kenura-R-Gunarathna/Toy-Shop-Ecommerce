About Us

Mythicon Co.

Kind Relationship Around The Galaxy.

Mythicon Co. is a growing company which creates technology- based toys with the aim of teaching kids to code while bringing fun to their playtime. By taking the form of mythical creatures such as Unicorns, Dragons etc., the Mythicon robot® toy allows kids to control their robot’s movements, lights & sounds by entering different codes through the “Mythicode” app. (Can download the app on compatible smart phones & tablets for free).

Designed using colorful LED lights, sensors & sounds, these toys offer a variety of fun coding challenges, improving their programming skills.

At present, Mythicon Co. has introduced two different robot mythical creatures, the Mythicon Unicorn & the Mythicon Dragon. Each product comes with different color sets & different sizes.

In the Mythicode app, beginners can start out using basic coding commands & can encounter with complexed codes as they level up.Completing coding challenges can unlock various levels while completing special missions can unlock secret codes. This allows kids to learn about new programs.
